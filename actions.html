About The Project

Enhancing STEM Education in Rural Pakistan

This initiative aims to establish state-of-the-art computer and science laboratories at Jajanwala Elementary School to provide quality education to students from 6th to 8th grade.

Our comprehensive approach includes:

  • Establishing fully equipped computer and science labs
  • Providing trained teachers to execute our specialized teaching plan
  • Implementing a curriculum focused on practical, hands-on learning
  • Regular monitoring and assessment to ensure program effectiveness

With the necessary NOC from the government, we are committed to transforming the educational landscape in this community and empowering students with 21st-century skills.
