Quality Education Makes a Nation

Our struggle

1: We are continuously consulting with teachers, students,
   and other relevant personalities.
2: In light of these conversations, we launched a pilot project.
   In this project, we selected a group of students and provided 
   them with computers and teachers to assess the capabilities and 
   approach of our 8th & 9th-grade students.
3: Following the success of this project, we are expanding it on a
   larger scale. So far, we are working with schools and private 
   centers, where we provide guides and computers to teach basic 
   AI knowledge and information about the latest technologies.
4: We are also organizing workshops for teachers.
5: Raising awareness in society about the latest technologies is 
   our priority to create change. We are constantly working to find 
   new ways to achieve our goal.
